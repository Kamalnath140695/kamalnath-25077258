Tamil Favourite Movies

  1. Bison

    • Heart-touching real story that connects with emotions
    • Excellent character development and storytelling
    • Outstanding performance by the lead actor

  2. Baasha

    • Mass movie that became a legend in Tamil cinema history
    • Rajinikanth's iconic performance and dialogue delivery
    • Perfect blend of action, emotion, and entertainment

  3. Vikram

    • Brilliant direction by Lokesh Kanagaraj
    • Masterful storytelling with interconnected plots
    • Exceptional action sequences and cinematography

  4. Kaithi

    • Deep philosophical themes about humanity and love
    • Kamal Haasan's brilliant performance and screenplay
    • Timeless message that remains relevant today

    • A.R. Rahman's debut and iconic music composition
    • Patriotic theme presented in an emotional way
    • Beautiful cinematography showcasing Kashmir's beauty
